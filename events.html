VISIT MALAYSIA 2025

Malaysia is a harmonious blend of Malay, Chinese, Indian, and the Bumiputera communities of Sabah and Sarawak.

  • The Malay community, the largest group, practices Islamic traditions and uses Bahasa Malaysia as the national language.
  • The Malaysian Chinese community contributes to the nation's modernity while preserving heritage through festivals like Lunar New Year
  • The Malaysian Indiancommunity enriches the country with vibrant spirituality, art, and colorful festivals such as Deepavali.

    • Meanwhile, the indigenous Bumiputera groups of Sabah and Sarawak including the Kadazan-Dusun, Iban, and Dayak embody Malaysia's indigenous heritage through unique customs, traditional dances like the Sumazau, and a deep connection to nature.

    This diversity makes Malaysia a captivating destination to experience unity in diversity!
